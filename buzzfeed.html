BuzzFeed Quizzes: The Windows to Our Soul

An exploration of identity, culture, and humor through viral internet quizzes.

By Tanya Budhrani

GitHub LinkedIn

“What kind of bread are you?” may seem like an absurd question, but BuzzFeed quizzes have become a cultural staple. They reflect our desires to categorize, understand, and project ourselves.

In this essay, we explore how the seemingly frivolous quizzes mirror deep social and psychological dynamics in the digital age, serving as both entertainment and identity exploration.
